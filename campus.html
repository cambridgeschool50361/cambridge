Campus & Facilities

Cambridge School provides world-class facilities for academic excellence, creativity, innovation, sportsmanship and personality development.

Explore Our Facilities

Art & Craft

Students in the art classes make the art room lively with paints, colours, cloth thread and various creative materials. Students learn sketching, painting, thread & fibre work and creative utilization of waste materials to develop artistic talent and imagination.

Explore More

Library

Our school library has a rich collection of academic books, fiction books and reference materials. Extended reading programmes are promoted for personality development, creativity and intellectual growth. Books provide knowledge, encouragement and lifelong learning.

Explore More

Computer Lab

The school has advanced computer labs equipped with internet facilities. Students gain both theoretical and practical knowledge while preparing presentations and digital projects under expert supervision. Modern technology learning prepares students for the future.

Explore More

Music

Music education develops emotional, intellectual and social growth. It enhances creativity, critical thinking and personality traits. Students participate in musical activities that inspire confidence, expression and cultural appreciation.

Explore More

Sports Facilities

The school provides excellent indoor and outdoor sports facilities including chess, carrom, table tennis, cricket, basketball, football, volleyball, badminton and athletics. Sports improve discipline, confidence, fitness, teamwork and leadership skills.

Explore More
