Mrs. Surekha Maheshwari

Principal, Cambridge School Dabra

A hearty welcome to Cambridge School. The aim of the school is to perpetuate & inculcate good ideas & values in younger generation. Its motto is to grow day by day every student physically healthy, mentally strong, intellectually sharp, emotionally balanced & spiritually aligned. The mind & spirit of a child has to be awakened to look for, search and learn whatever is new to him.

It provides best opportunity for students to develop academic skills and expand knowledge. Our endeavour is to train children to become autonomous learners. Our focus is to provide students with an education that allows them to recognize & realize their 'dream' & skills & to be able to ignite passion to excel. Students' be given an environment where their creativity developed & spontaneity nurtured. This school enables a child to grow into an adult who displays sensitivity, integrity, good citizenship & respect for human diversity and develops in a stress free environment " Education is an ornament in prosperity & refuge in adversity".
