School Rules & Regulations

Please follow all the rules carefully to maintain discipline and decorum in the school.

Every student must carry his/her school diary to school daily.

Students coming on their own should arrive before the school bell rings.

Changing classrooms between periods should be done silently and orderly.

Students must remain neat, clean and properly dressed in complete school uniform on all working days and school functions.

Boys must maintain proper haircuts and girls should tie their hair with black ribbon/band.

No books, magazines or newspapers other than textbooks and library books should be brought to school.

Lending or borrowing money and other articles is strictly prohibited.

Students escorted to school must wait for their escort before leaving the campus.

Parents and guardians should meet teachers during PTA meetings to know the progress of their wards.

Parents must not visit students or teachers without prior permission from the Principal.

Students involved in indisciplinary activities or damaging school property will face strict action.

Students found in classrooms during PT, Library or Morning Assembly will be severely dealt with.

Girls are not allowed to wear jewellery or apply nail polish.

Birthday celebrations should only be limited to distribution of toffees.

Students should keep to the left while climbing stairs.

Students absent without leave application for seven consecutive days will have their names struck off.

Presentation of gifts to teachers is not allowed.

Boys are not allowed to wear low waist pants or keep long hair.

Minimum 75% attendance is compulsory for all students.

Important Notice

Students are expected to maintain discipline, punctuality and respect towards teachers, classmates and school property at all times.
