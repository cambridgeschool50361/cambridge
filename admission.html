Admission Procedure

Begin Your Child's Journey Towards Excellence

Admissions Open 2026-27

Simple, transparent and student-friendly admission process.

Registration

Registration is prior to admission. The registration form enclosed with the prospectus must be carefully filled in and submitted within the prescribed time along with the registration fee.

Please note that registration does not necessarily guarantee admission. Admission depends on the availability of seats in the concerned class and various other admission criteria decided by the school management.

Admission

Admissions for the academic session commence from the first week of February. Normally, admissions are open for Nursery classes while admissions to other classes are subject to availability of seats.

The recommended age for admission to Nursery is that the child should complete 3 years on or before 1st April of the admission year.

Admission Process

1

Collect Prospectus

Obtain the school prospectus and registration form from the school office.

2

Submit Registration Form

Fill in the registration form carefully and submit it with the required fee and documents.

3

Admission Confirmation

Admission will be confirmed based on seat availability and completion of admission formalities.

Important Information

Parents are requested to complete all admission formalities within the given time. Incomplete forms or delayed submissions may not be considered.
