Mr. Udayveer Singh

Chairperson, Cambridge School Dabra

The school is based on the principle of generating awareness to the children, of their own responsibility and its all-round development on physical, intellectual, psychological and social aspects. Emphasis will be on synergising multi-farious activities for the children to create in them the 'Burning Desire' to learn and achieve value-based goals. Some of the goals have been given in the first page of the diary highlighting what the Cambridge school means.

To forge ahead in this direction, I would like to seek whole hearted co-operation of all of you to assist us in converting children's burning desire into action-oriented situations for development of their personality. Then only the children will succeed in becoming confident, competent and disciplined citizens without any fear or emotional aggression. May I request you to kindly continue interaction with us through the medium of diary and monthly parent teacher meeting to monitor the progress of your children and ensure further improvement. Wishing you all a very rewarding and successful year.
